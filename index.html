A little About Me

My journey into tech took an active turn early in 2009 when I co-founded Hedge&Cliff Concepts Ltd. Before this time I had what I would describe as a curious but inactive interest in software engineering. My interest mostly came from interacting with family and friends in the industry.

I was employed in the banking industry at the time, and had acquired a few years' experience in Customer Services, Customer Experience Management, Contact Center Services and much more. So, in 2009, I teamed up with a close friend and colleague to start a company where we would leverage our experience to make these same services available to companies in the MSME space.

We, therefore, had to source for the best tools and technology which would provide the most realistic blend of cost and efficiency for potential customers in our chosen business niche. This need became the trigger that propelled me onto my current path.

Moving on from Hedge&Cliff Concepts Ltd, I have gone on to acquire skills and experience in Software Product Management and Software Engineering. My current role as Director of Products at Scelloo Technologies, and it's subsidiaries, is a direct consequence of my decision to pursue a career in tech.

But I don't work or write code all the time, and here are some of the things I do as hobbies when I'm not working.
